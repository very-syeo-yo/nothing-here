Boy Pablo (stylized as boy pablo) is a Norwegian indie pop-rock project led by Chilean–Norwegian singer/songwriter and multi-instrumentalist Nicolas Muñoz. As a live band, boy pablo also consists of Muñoz’s friends Gabriel Muñoz, Eric Tryland, Henrik Åmdal, and Sigmund Vestrheim.
